Comments:
 1.  Format of the observations of Phobos and Deimos in
     files  mm0001 - mm0017 is similar to that described in
     Morley T.A. (1989)
     A catalogue of ground-based astrometric observations of the Martian
     satellites, 1877-1982.
     Astronomy and Astrophysics Supplement Series. V. 77. N. 2. P. 209-226.
 2.  The data in  mm0019  are the results of reprocessing of
     previously published observations with the modern star
     catalogues. These prevously results are included in
     the portions  mm0001, mm0011.
 *3. In the portions 0031, 0032 the data are similar but Mars Express positions were: 
     portion 0031 - retrieved from the European Space Operations Centre (ESOC) navigation orbits,
     portion 0032 - generated at the European Space Astronomy Centre (ESAC).
