Comments.
  1. This portion includes most observations published earlier in the portion um0130. In particular, it includes all
     observations of Titania and Oberon available in um0130, as well as one observation of Umbriel. The coordinates
     are slightly different.
  2. Some observations of Umbriel, Titania and Oberon are also available in the portion um0118. The coordinates are
     slightly different.
  3. It is not clear which ephemerides were used to obtain O-C values.

Format.
  1. Year   of the date of observation
  2. Month  of the date of observation
  3. Day    of the date of observation with decimals
  4. Index of the satellite (N): 1 - U1, 2 - U2, etc.
  5. Hour   of right ascension (alpha, h)
  6. Minute of right ascension (alpha, m)
  7. Second of right ascension with decimals (alpha, s)
  8. Degree of declination (delta, deg)
  9. Minute of declination (delta, '  )
 10. Second of declination with decimals (delta, '' )
 11. (O-C) in right ascension, (O-C)a (arcsec)
 12. (O-C) in declination, (O-C)d (arcsec)
 13. Exposure time (ExT, sec)
 14. Index of emulsion (I): 1 - ORWO ZU21, 2 - Agfa Astro, 3 - ORWO NP27
 15. Photographic magnitude (Bmag)
 16. Photographic magnitude R.M.S. error (e_Bmag)
 17. Right ascension R.M.S. error (e_RAs, arcsec)
 18. Declination R.M.S. error (e_DEs, arcsec)
 19. Number of reference stars (Nz)
 20. Telescope (T): 1 - DL, 2 - DW, 3 - Z6
 21. Unique identifier of the plate (NPl)
