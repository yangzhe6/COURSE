Comments.
 The satellite was detected during the stellar occultation by (523764) 2014 WC510 observed on 2018 December 1.
 This occultation campaign was part of the Research and Education Collaborative Occultation Network (RECON), a
 network of small telescopes spread over 2000 km in western USA and Canada.
