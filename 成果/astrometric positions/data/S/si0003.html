Note (1): Observation type:
      0: RA, DE
      1: {Delta}RA*cosDE, {Delta}DE
      2: {Delta}RA, {Delta}DE
      3: position angle, separation
Note (2): Satellite numbers:
      0 for Saturn,
      1 to 8 for S1 to S8, 12 for Helene, 13 for Telesto, 14 for Calypso,
      C for the center of the plate which is not a physical object.
Note (3): Obs1 and Obs2 are in arcsec but in degree when Sat = C*
      When Obstype=0, Obs1=RA, Obs2=DE
      When ObsType=1, Obs1={Delta}RA*cosDE,Obs2={Delta}DE
Note (4): Reference system code:
      0: mean equator and equinox B1950,
      1: true equator and equinox of date of observation,
      2: mean equator and equinox of J2000
Note (5): Reference frame code:
      0: topocentric,
      1: geocentric,
      2: heliocentric
Note (6): Residuals of observations:
      999.999 when the residuals are not computed
      888.888 when the residuals are greater than 100 in absolute value
