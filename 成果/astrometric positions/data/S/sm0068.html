Format.

   1. image name
   2. Year / Gregorian date
   3. Month / Gregorian date
   4. Day / Gregorian date
   5. Time in hr min sec.decimals (UTC) 
   6. Day fraction
   7. Number of satellite (N sat): 1 - Mimas, 2 - Enceladus, 3 - Tethys,
                                   4 - Dione, 5 - Rhea, 8 - Iapetus.
   8. Right ascension (degrees)
   9. Declination (degrees)
  10. Sigma Right ascension (degrees) 
  11. Sigma Declination (degrees)
  12. Residuals Right ascension (arcseconds)
  13. Residuals Declination (arcseconds) 
  14. Residuals Right ascension (km) 
  15. Residuals Declination (km) 
  16. Stars number
