Comments.
  The observations in this portion of data (taken from VizieR) are almost identical to those in the portion sm0074 (taken from the MAO NASU Web-site).
  In comparison with sm0074, this portion has more observations although some observations available in sm0074 were omitted or corrected.

Format.
  1. Year   of the date of observation
  2. Month  of the date of observation
  3. Day    of the date of observation with decimals
  4. Number of the satellite (N): S2 - Enceladus, S3 - Tethys,  etc.
  5. Hour   of right ascension (alpha, h)
  6. Minute of right ascension (alpha, m)
  7. Second of right ascension with decimals (alpha, s)
  8. Degree of declination (delta, deg)
  9. Minute of declination (delta, '  )
 10. Second of declination with decimals (delta, '' )
 11. Photographic B magnitude (Bmag)
 12. Exposure time (ExpT, s)
 13. Number of reference stars (Nz)
 14. Bmag R.M.S. error (e_Bm)
 15. Right ascension R.M.S. error (e_RAs, arcsec)
 16. Declination R.M.S. error (e_DEs, arcsec)
 17. Emulsion (E): 1 - ORWO ZU2, 2 - ORWO ZU21, 3 - ORWO NP27, 4 - Agfa Astro, 5 - KodakU,IIaO
 18. Telescope (T): 1 - DL, 2 - DW, 3 - DZ, 4 - Z6
 19. Unique identifier of the plate (WFPDBid)
