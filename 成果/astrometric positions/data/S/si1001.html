Format.
  1. Number of the satellite (N sat) (see above)
  2. Year   of the moment of observation
  3. Month  of the moment of observation
  4. Day    of the moment of observation with decimals
  5. Apparent distance from the center of planet
     (minus if the right ascension of satellite is less than
      r.a. of the planet)
  6. Type of the value (0 - km, 1 - arcsec)
  7. Number of observatory from the list above
  8. Number of receptor from the list above
  9. Number of telescope from the list above
 10. Number of observer from the list above
 11. O-C - residuals as compared with the theory of
     Nicholson et al.(1983, Icarus, v. 100, p. 464-484)
     realised by N.Emelianov
 12. O-C1 - residuals published by Seidelmann et al.(1981, Icarus,
            v. 47, p. 282-287), the ambiguous value -99.0 is set
            if the data is absent
 13. O-C2 - residuals published by Yoder et al.(1989, Astron. J.,
            v. 98, no. 5, p. 1875-1889), the ambiguous value
            -99.0 is set if the data is absent
 14. Number of reference from the list above
