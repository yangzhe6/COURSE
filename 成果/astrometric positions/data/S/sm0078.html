Comments.

Format.
   1. Image identification number (Id)
   2. Year of the date of the observation
   3. Month of the date of the observation
   4. Day of the date of the observation
   5. Hour of the observation time (image mid-time)
   6. Minute of the observation time (image mid-time)
   7. Seconds of the observation time (image mid-time)
   8. Right ascension of the camera's pointing vector orientation (RAc, degrees)
   9. Declination of the camera's pointing vector orientation (DEc, degrees)
  10. The camera's twist angle (twist, degrees)
  11. The satellite's line position (line, pixels)
  12. The satellite's sample position (sample, pixels)
  13. The satellite's right ascension (RA, degrees)
  14. The satellite's declination (DE, degrees)
  15. The satellite's identification number, N (1 — S1, 2 — S2, etc.)
